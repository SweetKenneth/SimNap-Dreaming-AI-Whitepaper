# 4. DEFINITIONS  
**Dream Cycle:**  
An internally triggered autonomous generative sequence performed without human prompting.

**Reflection Loop:**  
A cognitive pass where the system analyzes prior dream output.

**Subconscious Layering:**  
Hidden internal processes that recombine latent concepts before dream output occurs.

**Behavioral Rhythm:**  
A recurring temporal signature of internal cognition (e.g., 00:00 reflection, 06:00 consolidation).

**Identity Continuity:**  
The system’s ability to maintain a persistent cognitive thread across days.

**Autonomous Reasoning:**  
The system’s capability to form conclusions without external prompts.

**Internal Drift Compensation:**  
The automatic correction of timing misalignment within cycles.

**Dream Seeds:**  
Internally generated prompts initiating dream cycles.
